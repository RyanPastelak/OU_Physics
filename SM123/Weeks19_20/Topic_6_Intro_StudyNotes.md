## Key Concepts

- **Nuclear Physics**: Study of atomic nuclei, their constituents and interactions
- **Particle Physics**: Study of fundamental particles and their interactions
- **Radioactive Decay**: Spontaneous transformation of unstable nuclei
- **Half-life**: Time for half of radioactive atoms to decay
- **Nuclear Fission**: Heavy nuclei splitting apart
- **Nuclear Fusion**: Light nuclei joining together
- **Quarks and Leptons**: Fundamental particles of matter
- **Strong and Weak Interactions**: Fundamental forces governing particle behaviour
