Hi everyone,

I chose Earth to look at as it wasn't so far for me to travel for accurate observations ;).

I calculated total energy values of -2.65 x 1033 Joules to 3 significant figures for both perihelion and aphelion albeit with varying values for kinetic and potential energies.

It appears there is a pattern forming where total energies at both points in the orbit are equal for all planets.

I found this intriguing and looked further for an explanation. Essentially it appears the closed elliptical orbit of our planet will have an equal amount of total energy regardless of it's position and despite the changes in kinetic and potential energy. 

As no energy is required to overcome opposing forces in the vacuum of space, the total energy must remain constant. So when the Earth is closer to the sun at perihelion it has sped up, gaining kinetic energy, but is closer to the sun and so loses potential energy. The reverse of this must be true at aphelion.

Looking forward to seeing values for Mars and hoping this made some sort of sense smile

Hi Alexandra,

great we had the same figure, I was hoping someone else would look at earth too smile.

I see what you mean and I think you're spot on with energy conservation being a general principle. The sentence implies energy conservation happens because there are no opposing forces, whereas energy conservation holds regardless, provided no external non-conservative forces do net work on the system (such as the skier experiencing wind resistance and friction). 

In a closed two-body system such as the Earth‚ÄìSun system, the only force acting is gravity, which is a conservative force. According to Newton‚Äôs laws, this force changes the Earth‚Äôs velocity (and therefore its kinetic energy) as its distance from the Sun changes, but it does not remove energy from the system. As a result, energy is continually transferred between kinetic and gravitational potential energy while the total mechanical energy remains constant.

I guess a better sentence would be along the lines of - 

Because the Earth‚ÄìSun system is isolated and only conservative gravitational forces act, Newton‚Äôs laws predict that the total mechanical energy of the orbit remains constant, even though kinetic and potential energies vary with distance.

I hope that helps a bit and we always should question and clarify as scientists! smile
